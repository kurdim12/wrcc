The team conceived the system, designed the hardware (enclosure, double-lumen
injector probe, bayonet mount — see the mechanical dossier) and the multi-sensor
architecture, and wrote and understand the software. AI coding assistants were
used as tools (like an IDE or compiler); every engineering decision is the team's
own and the team can explain and modify any part of the codebase.
