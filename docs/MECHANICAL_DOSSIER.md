# Palm Guard — Mechanical Design (V4 hardware)

Summary of the V4 mechanical design (full CAD dossier: `PalmGuardMechanicalDossier.pdf`).
Modelled in Autodesk Fusion (direct modelling); demo medium is **clear water only**.

**Three FDM-printed parts, one sealed instrument:**

- **Enclosure** — ≈ 80 × 67 × 103 mm, 2.5 mm walls / 2.8 mm base, reinforced
  corners (outer R8 / inner R5.5 → uniform **2.45 mm** wall, no weak edge).
  Houses the electronics + power chain; side **USB-C service slot**; rear
  cantilever snap.
- **Lid** — 15° forward-tilted **solar tray** + plug-in sealing rim, cantilever
  snap (no fasteners, field-removable; verified snap retention).
- **Probe** — seamless **tangent-ogive** needle (≈ 50 mm reach), **double-lumen**:
  a treatment lumen (clear water in the demo) venting through 2 × Ø0.9 mm side
  ports, and a **sealed sensor lumen** carrying the trunk-temperature sensor.
- **Mount** — front-loading **quarter-turn bayonet**: the probe is the one
  field-serviceable wear part, swappable from outside without opening the sealed
  electronics.

Fits verified in CAD (0.00 cm³ probe ∩ body interference; uniform corner walls;
snap retention checked). Trunk injection (endotherapy) is a recognised real-world
RPW treatment route; the booth demonstration uses **clear water only**, delivered
only after human confirmation.
